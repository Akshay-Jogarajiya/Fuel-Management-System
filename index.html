Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my fuel data?

Simply go to the "Add Fuel Data" section and input the necessary information after each refuel.

Can I export my fuel reports?

Yes, you can export your reports as PDFs or Excel files under the "View Reports" section.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data, ensuring that it is safe and private.

Can I use this app for multiple vehicles?

Yes, you can add multiple vehicles to your account and track each one individually.
